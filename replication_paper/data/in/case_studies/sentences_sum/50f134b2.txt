 The Cuban missile crisis began when the Soviet Union sent ballistic missiles to Cuba in response to the U.S. placing missiles in Turkey . The Soviet Union responded by sending missiles into Cuba .
 For 13 days in October 1962, the U.S. and Soviet Union stared down the barrel of mutually assured destruction . The United States and the Soviet Union faced each other for 13 days .
 In late September 1962, U.S. spy planes flying over Cuba discovered the presence of Soviet-made nuclear missiles on the island . The missiles were discovered by the United States .
