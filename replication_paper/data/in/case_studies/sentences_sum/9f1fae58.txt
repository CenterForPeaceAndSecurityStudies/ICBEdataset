 Reports from mid October 1962 confirmed that the Soviet Union was installing intermediate-range nuclear missiles in Cuba . The missiles were located ninety miles off the shore of Florida .
 McNamara recalls this pivotal moment in the Cuban missile crisis . McNamara reads from the first of two letters that Nikita Khrushchev sent President John F Kennedy .
 The period from November 1962 to the end of the Cuban missile crisis is generally seen as the most dangerous period of the entire Cold War . The period between November 1962 and December 1962 is considered the most serious period of Cold War history .
