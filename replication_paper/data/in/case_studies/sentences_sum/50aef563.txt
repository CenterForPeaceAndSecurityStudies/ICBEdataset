 Nikita Khrushchev agreed to Cuba's request to place nuclear missiles on the island to deter a future invasion . The move was a response to the failed Bay of Pigs Invasion of 1961 and the presence of American Jupiter ballistic missiles in Italy and Turkey .
 The White House denied charges for months that it was ignoring dangerous Soviet missiles 90 miles from Florida . Meanwhile, the 1962 U.S. elections were underway, and the White House had denied charges .
 Kennedy ordered a naval blockade on October 22 to prevent further missiles from reaching Cuba . Kennedy ordered the blockade after consultation with the Cuban government .
 After several days of tense negotiations, an agreement was reached between Kennedy and Khrushchev . Publicly, the Soviets would dismantle their offensive weapons in Cuba and return them to the Soviet Union, subject to United Nations verification, in exchange for a U.S. public declaration .
 The United States agreed that it would dismantle all US-built Jupiter MRBMs, which had been deployed in Turkey . There has been debate on whether Italy was included in the agreement as well .
