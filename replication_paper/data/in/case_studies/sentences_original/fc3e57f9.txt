Description: TIMELINE OF EVENTS PRECEDING THIS AUDIO RESOURCE: Monday, October 15: A U-2 reconnaissance aircraft, piloted by Richard Heyser, reveals several SS-4 nuclear missiles in Cuba.Tuesday, October 16: After learning of the missiles during breakfast, President Kennedy convenes his Executive Committee to consider America’s options.Wednesday, October 17 – Friday, October 19: Amid scheduled campaign trips to Connecticut and the Midwest, President Kennedy meets with and advises Soviet Foreign Minister Andrie Gromyko that America will not tolerate Soviet missiles in Cuba.
Although it may seem that the events of the seven days between October 22 and 28 unfolded at a blinding pace, the entire incident — which has come to be collectively known as the “Cuban missile crisis” — was the culmination of a longer process.
